LinBo Wang   

Associate Professor, School of Computer Science & Technology, Anhui University

Email: wanglb@ahu.edu.cn

I am a associate professor of the School of Computer Science and Technology at Anhui University.

My research interests are in the broad areas of deep learning, computer vision, and image processing. Some of the projects I involoved have codes opened in the GitHub, while more complete versions of the open codes and my published papers can be found from the Google Scholar.
